# Key Insights from Pizza Sales Analysis

- Large-sized pizzas contribute the highest share of total revenue,
  indicating a customer preference for larger portion sizes.

- Order volume is highest during evening hours, particularly between
  6 PM and 8 PM, suggesting peak demand during dinner time.

- A small number of pizza types generate a significant portion of
  overall revenue, following a Pareto-like distribution.

- Classic category pizzas are the most frequently ordered, making them
  the strongest performing category in terms of demand.

- Revenue trends over time show a steady cumulative growth, indicating
  consistent sales throughout the period analyzed.

- Category-wise ranking reveals that top-performing pizzas differ
  across categories, highlighting the importance of targeted product
  analysis rather than overall rankings alone.
